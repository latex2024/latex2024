About LaTeX 2024

The Department of Mathematics, National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur (NIT Jamshedpur) is proud to organize the Short Term Course on LaTeX Programming (LATEX 2024) in online / virtual mode during April 01-05, 2024. The main objective of LaTeX 2024is to make the participants understand about LaTeX system, explain procedure of LaTeX typesetting, familiarize the participants with various document formats of LaTeX. The program will also include practice sessions. All major concepts of LaTeX typesetting will be covered during the training program which will be helpful for preparing research articles, thesis and books, and presentations.

Course Contents

Basic Tools for Formatting Texts
Commands and Environments, Error Messages
Mathematical Formulas, Class and Package Files
Document Layout and Organization, Displayed Text
Graphics Packages
Bibliographic Databases
Research Article, Book, Thesis, Presentation (Beamer)
